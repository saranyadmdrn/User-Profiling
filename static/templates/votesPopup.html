Analytics on votes

  • The graph shows consolidated view of the user's vote.
  • With higher down votes, users can know that the post is not popular and is misleading
  • The higher the upvotes for an answer, the user found what he was looking for.
  • In the future, those upvoted posts can be suggested to users looking for similar answers.
  • The user can see if other users are finding more relevant answers than him by looking at upvotes for answers.
